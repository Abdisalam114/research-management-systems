QAYBTA 1 — OVERVIEW / GUUD AHAAN
================================================================================

English: JUST RMS manages research from funding call → proposal → multi-stage review → project → finance → publications → closure.

Somali: JUST RMS wuxuu maamulaa cilmi-baarista: funding call → proposal → dib u eegis → project → maaliyad → publications → xiritaan.

English: Two portals — Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG). Data never mixes across portals.

Somali: Laba portal — Undergraduate (UG) iyo Postgraduate (PG). Xogtu isma dhex gasho.

English: Only the Research Director can switch portals. Everyone else stays on one portal.

Somali: Kaliya Research Director ayaa beddela portal. Dadka kale hal portal ayay ku jiraan.

English: The app UI language is English. This guide is bilingual (English + Somali).

Somali: Interface-ka waa Ingiriisi. Tilmaamahan waa laba luuqad.

QAYBTA 2 — USER JOBS / SHAQADA MID KASTA (8 ROLES)
================================================================================

There is no separate Ethics Committee or Peer Reviewer account. Ethics is approved by the Research Director. Peer review is done by University Leadership (score + comment).

Ma jiro akoon gooni ah oo Ethics Committee ama Peer Reviewer. Ethics-ka waxaa ansixiya Research Director. Peer review waxaa sameeya University Leadership.


1. Research Director — director@rms.edu

EN — Job / what they do:
  • Switch Undergraduate / Postgraduate portals
  • Create and publish funding calls (Internal / External) — no Leadership step
  • Final Approve / Reject on proposals
  • Decide ethics (JUREC) applications
  • Approve purchase orders after Finance review
  • Manage Users and Departments
  • View analytics, KPI, audit trail, all modules

SO — Shaqada:
  • Beddel portal UG / PG
  • Abuuri oo daabaca funding calls (Internal / External) — Leadership uma baahna
  • Ansixinta ugu dambeysa ee proposals
  • Go'aanka ethics (JUREC)
  • Ansixinta PO ka dib Finance dib-u-eegis
  • Maamul Users iyo Departments
  • Analytics, KPI, audit trail


2. University Leadership — leadership@rms.edu / leadership.pg@rms.edu

EN — Job:
  • Peer review assigned proposals: score 1–5 + comment
  • Open Peer Reviews and Proposals menus
  • View KPI dashboard and funding calls (read — does not publish calls)
  • Does NOT approve/publish funding calls

SO — Shaqada:
  • Peer review: score 1–5 + comment
  • Peer Reviews iyo Proposals
  • KPI dashboard; funding calls wuu arki karaa (ma daabaco)
  • MA ansixiyo / daabaco funding calls


3. Faculty Coordinator — coordinator@rms.edu / coordinator.pg@rms.edu

EN — Job:
  • Review faculty proposal queue
  • Create and manage thesis groups (min 4 students), assign supervisors, accept titles
  • Advance publication faculty workflow stages
  • Validate publications (Validate → Published)
  • Faculty reports / monitoring dashboard
  • View projects, ethics list, audit trail

SO — Shaqada:
  • Dib u eegista proposal-yada faculty-ga
  • Kooxaha thesis (min 4 arday), supervisors, ansixi title
  • Horumarinta publication workflow
  • Validate publications → Published
  • Warbixinta / dashboard faculty
  • Projects, ethics, audit trail


4. Finance Officer — finance@rms.edu / finance.pg@rms.edu

EN — Job (MONEY + PO REVIEW):
  • Grant funding approval → Authorize budget (allocate only, not pay)
  • Finance review of proposal budgets
  • Manage budgets, payment requests, and pay approved items
  • Review Purchase Orders (approve/reject) BEFORE Director
  • Pay purchase orders AFTER Director approved the PO
  • Finance reports and donor financial reports
  • Project closure (Finance) approval
  • Flow: Researcher creates PO → Finance reviews → Director approves → Finance pays

SO — Shaqada (LACAG + DIB U EEGISTA PO):
  • Grant funding approval → Authorize budget (qoondee kaliya, ha bixin weli)
  • Dib u eegista budget-ka proposal-ka
  • Budgets, payment requests, bixinta lacagta
  • Dib u eegis Purchase Orders (ansixi/diid) ka hor Director
  • Bixi PO ka dib Director ansixiyo
  • Finance reports / donor reports
  • Xiritaan maaliyadeed (Project closure Finance)
  • Safka: Researcher PO → Finance review → Director → Finance bixisaa

Procurement Officer role was removed — Finance owns PO review.


5. HR Officer — hr@rms.edu / hr.pg@rms.edu

EN — Job:
  • View projects, thesis groups, and research groups for HR context
  • Support staffing / people side of research projects
  • Does not approve grants, ethics, or payments

SO — Shaqada:
  • Arag projects, thesis, groups — dhinaca HR
  • Taageero dadka / kooxaha mashruuca
  • Ma ansixiyo grants, ethics, ama lacag bixin


7. Donor Agency — donor@rms.edu / donor.pg@rms.edu

EN — Job:
  • Create external funding call drafts
  • Wait for Research Director to publish (Leadership not required)
  • Monitor open/closed calls and donor reports
  • Read-only awareness of related grants

SO — Shaqada:
  • Abuuri funding calls dibadda ah (draft)
  • Sug Research Director publish (Leadership uma baahna)
  • La soco calls iyo donor reports
  • Arag grants la xiriira


8. Researcher / PI — asha@rms.edu (UG) / mahad@rms.edu (PG)

EN — Job:
  • Apply to funding calls; create proposals (budget + ethics + docs)
  • Run approved projects: milestones, work plan, progress, communication log
  • Create budgets and payment requests; create purchase orders
  • Register publications (Create & submit); upload repository files
  • Supervise thesis groups (enter title, meetings, chapters)
  • Submit project closure checklist

SO — Shaqada:
  • Codso funding calls; abuuri proposals (budget + ethics + docs)
  • Maamul projects: milestones, work plan, progress, communication log
  • Abuuri budgets, payment requests, iyo purchase orders
  • Publications (Create & submit); repository files
  • Thesis supervision (title, kulamo, chapters)
  • Gudbi closure checklist

QAYBTA 5 — FUNDING CALLS / DEEQAHA
================================================================================

A.1 Create a funding call — Director or Donor

English — Research Director:
  1. Sidebar → Funding Calls → + New funding call
  2. Choose Call type: Internal seed grant OR External / donor-funded
  3. Set Eligible researchers: All (UG & PG) / UG only / PG only / PGD
  4. Required documents fill automatically — edit if needed
  5. Save as draft → Publish yourself (Leadership is not required)

Somali — Research Director:
  1. Funding Calls → + New funding call
  2. Dooro Internal ama External
  3. Dooro cidda codsan karta: All / UG / PG / PGD
  4. Requirements si otomaatig ah ayaa u buuxsamaan
  5. Save draft → Publish adiga (Leadership uma baahna)

English — Donor Agency:
  • External calls only (draft). Research Director publishes — no Leadership step.

Somali — Donor:
  • External kaliya (draft). Research Director ayaa daabaca — Leadership uma baahna.

A.2 Researchers apply

English:
  1. Login as researcher (asha@ UG or mahad@ PG)
  2. Notifications → New funding call open → Open (deep-links to that call)
  3. Funding Calls → Apply via this call

Somali:
  1. Gal researcher ahaan
  2. Notifications → call furan → Open (wuxuu ku geeyaa call-ka saxda ah)
  3. Funding Calls → Apply via this call

A.3 Review proposal (funding-call path)

English — review stages:
  1. Admin screening
  2. Peer review — Leadership (Director assigns; Leadership submits score 1–5 + comment)
  3. Committee review
  4. Ethics — Research Director approves JUREC (no separate ethics role)
  5. Finance review (proposal budget)
  6. Director final Approve → Project created + grant queued for finance (pending_finance)

Somali — marxaladaha:
  1. Admin screening
  2. Peer review — Leadership score + comment
  3. Committee review
  4. Ethics — Director ansixiyo
  5. Finance review
  6. Director Approve → Project + grant finance queue

QAYBTA 6 — AUTHORIZE MONEY (FINANCE) / LACAGTA OGGOLAANSI
================================================================================

IMPORTANT / MUHIIM:
  Authorize budget ≠ Pay money.
  Authorize = allocate budget only. Pay later under Finance & Budgets.

English — after Director accepts a funding-call award:
  1. Grant status becomes pending_finance
  2. Finance Officer gets notification → Grant funding approval
  3. Sidebar → Grant funding approval (/finance/grant-approvals)
  4. Click Authorize budget
  5. Grant becomes active; budget allocated (Paid = 0 until disbursement)

Somali — ka dib marka Director aqbalo award-ka:
  1. Grant-ku wuxuu noqdaa pending_finance
  2. Finance wuxuu helaa notification → Grant funding approval
  3. Sidebar → Grant funding approval
  4. Authorize budget
  5. Grant active; budget allocated (Paid = 0 ilaa lacag la bixiyo)

English — if you cannot find Authorize:
  • Login as finance@rms.edu (UG) or finance.pg@rms.edu (PG)
  • Director/Researcher see a banner: Finance must authorize

Somali — haddii aadan helin Authorize:
  • Gal Finance Officer ahaan
  • Director/Researcher waxay arkaan tilmaam: Finance → Grant funding approval

QAYBTA 7 — PUBLICATIONS / NATIJOOYINKA
================================================================================

English:
  1. One research output per project (1:1)
  2. Researcher: Publications → Create & submit (or Save draft then Submit)
  3. Status becomes submitted → Faculty workflow stage Submitted
  4. Coordinator/Director: Validate on Publications page → status validated AND workflow Published
  5. Or advance stages on Research Workflow Status: Submitted → In process → Pipeline → Published
  6. Use the Published filter tile to see published outputs (not the Submitted filter)

Somali:
  1. Hal project = hal output
  2. Researcher: Publications → Create & submit
  3. Status = submitted → marxalad Submitted
  4. Coordinator/Director: Validate → validated + Published
  5. Ama Research Workflow → horumarinta marxaladaha ilaa Published
  6. Filter Published — ha isticmaalin Submitted haddii aad raadinayso kuwa la daabacay

QAYBTA 8 — FULL RESEARCH JOURNEY / SAFKA BUUXA
================================================================================

STEP 1 — Funding Call → Grant application
  EN: Open call → researcher applies
  SO: Call furan → researcher codsado

STEP 2 — Proposal
  EN: Proposals → New → title, abstract, budget, compliance docs, supporting docs, ethics form → Submit
  SO: Proposals → cusub → buuxi dhammaan → Submit

STEP 3 — Multi-stage review → Director Approve
  EN: Creates Project automatically; funding-call grants go to pending_finance
  SO: Project si otomaatig ah; grant-ku wuxuu galayaa finance queue

STEP 4 — Project execution
  EN: Milestones, work plan, activities, communication log, progress reports
  SO: Milestones, work plan, hawlaha, communication log, warbixinno

STEP 5 — Finance & purchase orders
  EN: Finance authorizes grant budget → Budget → payment requests → PO (Finance review → Director → Finance pay)
  SO: Finance authorize → Budget → lacag bixin → PO

STEP 6 — Outputs
  EN: Publications (Validate → Published), Research Workflow, Repository, Thesis (min 4 students per group)
  SO: Publications (Validate → Published), Repository, Thesis (ugu yaraan 4 arday)

STEP 7 — Closure
  EN: Checklist + final report + lessons learned → Director → Finance → Archive
  SO: Checklist + final report + lessons learned → Director → Finance → Archive

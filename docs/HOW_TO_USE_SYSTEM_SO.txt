1. GUUD AHAAN

JUST RMS wuxuu maamulaa cilmi-baarista jaamacadda: funding calls, proposals, dib u eegis, project, maaliyad, publications, iyo xiritaan.

Laba portal ayaa jira: Undergraduate (UG) iyo Postgraduate (PG). Kaliya Research Director ayaa beddeli kara labadooda. Xogtu isma dhex gasho.

Fur app-ka: http://localhost:5173 (ka dib backend iyo frontend la shido).


2. SHAQADA USER-KASTA (8 ROLES)

Ma jiro akoon Ethics Committee ama Peer Reviewer. Ethics = Research Director. Peer review = University Leadership.

Research Director (director@rms.edu)
  • Beddel portal UG/PG; abuuri/daabaca funding calls gudaha
  • Ansixinta ugu dambeysa proposals; go'aanka ethics (JUREC)
  • Ansixi PO ka dib Procurement; maamul Users & Departments
  • Analytics, KPI, audit trail

University Leadership (leadership@ / leadership.pg@)
  • Ansixi/daabaca funding calls
  • Peer review: score 1–5 + comment
  • Peer Reviews + Proposals; KPI

Faculty Coordinator (coordinator@ / coordinator.pg@)
  • Proposal queue faculty; thesis groups (min 4 arday), titles, supervisors
  • Publication workflow; Validate → Published
  • Warbixin / monitoring faculty

Finance Officer (finance@ / finance.pg@) — LACAG (ma aha Procurement)
  • Grant funding approval → Authorize budget (qoondee kaliya, ha bixin weli)
  • Finance review proposal; budgets; payments; bixi wax la ansixiyay
  • Bixi PO ka dib Director; finance reports; xiritaan maaliyadeed
  • MA go'aamin waxa la iibinayo (taasi waa Procurement)

Procurement Officer (procurement@ / procurement.pg@) — IIBSASHADA / PO (ma aha Finance)
  • Dib u eegis Purchase Orders ee researchers
  • Ansixi/diid PO ka hor Director
  • MA authorize grant; MA bixin lacag
  • Safka: Researcher PO → Procurement → Director → Finance bixisaa

HR Officer (hr@ / hr.pg@)
  • Arag projects, thesis, groups — dhinaca HR
  • Ma ansixiyo grants/ethics/lacag

Donor Agency (donor@ / donor.pg@)
  • Draft funding calls external; sug Leadership/Director publish
  • Donor reports; la soco grants

Researcher (asha@ UG / mahad@ PG)
  • Codso calls; abuuri proposals (budget + ethics + docs)
  • Maamul projects; budgets; payments; abuuri PO
  • Publications Create & submit; repository; thesis; closure checklist

4. FUNDING CALLS

Director wuxuu abuuraa Internal ama External. Requirements si otomaatig ah ayaa u buuxsamaan. Dooro eligibility (UG / PG / dhammaan / PGD).

Leadership ama Director publish → researchers eligible ah waa la ogeysiiyaa link toos ah call-ka.

Researcher: Funding Calls → Apply via this call.

Tusaale PG: mahad@rms.edu wuxuu helaa ogeysiinta haddii call-ku yahay PG ama All.


5. SAFKA DIB U EEGISTA

Admin screening → Leadership peer review (score 1–5 + comment) → Committee → Ethics (Research Director) → Finance review → Director Approve.

Proposal la aqbalay → Project la abuuraa + Grant finance queue (pending_finance).


6. LACAGTA OGGOLAANSI (FINANCE)

Waddada: Sidebar → Grant funding approval (/finance/grant-approvals)

Gal: finance@rms.edu (UG) ama finance.pg@rms.edu (PG)

Guji Authorize budget. Tani waa ALLOCATE kaliya — ma aha bixin lacag. Lacag bixinta waa Finance & Budgets ka dib.

Muhiim: Marka Director aqbalo award-ka, Finance wuxuu helaa notification.


7. PUBLICATIONS

Hal project = hal output. Researcher: Create & submit → submitted.

Coordinator/Director: Validate → validated iyo Published.

Ama Research Workflow: Submitted → In process → Pipeline → Published.

Filter Published — ha isticmaalin Submitted haddii aad raadinayso kuwa la daabacay.


8. SAFKA BUUXA

Funding call → Grant → Proposal (budget + ethics + docs) → Dib u eegis → Project → Finance authorize → Work plan + budgets → Lacag bixin + PO → Publications (Validate = Published) → Closure checklist → Archive.


9. MENU-YADA MUHIIMKA AH

Funding Calls, Grants, Proposals, Peer Reviews, Projects, Publications, Research Workflow, Finance & Budgets, Grant funding approval (Finance), Policies, Search, Notifications, Audit Trail.


10. SOO KOOBID HAL JUMLAD

Director daabaco call → Researcher codsado → Leadership peer review → Director ansixiyo ethics + proposal → Finance authorize budget → Project socdo → Validate = Published → Xiritaan.

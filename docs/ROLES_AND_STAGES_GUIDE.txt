1. HORDHAC (INTRODUCTION)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Jamhuriya RMS waa nidaam maamul oo cilmi-baarista jaamacadda u horseeda 
socodka: Proposal → Ethics → Review → Project → Grant → Budget → Publication → Repository.

Marka la galo, isticmaale wuxuu dooranayaa portal-ka:
  • Undergraduate (UG) — cilmi-baarista heerka Bachelor
  • Postgraduate (PG) — Master, PGD, iyo cilmi-baarista sare

QAYBTA A: ROLES-KA (DOORASHADA MAS’UULIYADDA)
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A.1 RESEARCH DIRECTOR (Agaasimaha Cilmi-baarista)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Magaca nidaamka: research_director

Sharaxaad:
Agaasimaha cilmi-baarista waa mas’uulka ugu sarreeya ee nidaamka RMS. Wuxuu 
leeyahay aragti guud (institutional oversight) dhammaan qaybaha cilmi-baarista 
jaamacadda.

Mas’uuliyadaha ugu muhiimsan:
  • Ansixinta isticmaaleyaasha cusub (User Approval — /pending-users)
  • Maamulka faculties & departments (/departments)
  • Ansixinta anshaxa REC (Ethics approval) — Director kaliya
  • Ansixinta PO ka dib Finance review
  • Go’aanka ugu dambeeya ee proposal-ka (approve / reject / revision)
  • Maamulka deeqaha (grants) — ansixin ama diidid
  • Aragtida analytics-ka guud ee jaamacadda (Dashboard)
  • Eegista Research Workflow Status ee dhammaan researchers-ka
  • Horumarinta marxaladaha publication workflow (Submitted → Published)
  • Maamulka thesis groups, repository, iyo modules-ka kale

Modules-ka uu geli karo:
Dashboard, Research Workflow Status, Ethics, Proposals, Projects, Publications, 
Thesis, Grants, Budgets, Repository, Collaboration, Groups, Messages, 
Notifications, Users, Departments, Profile.

Ma geli karo (si gaar ah):
Ma aha Finance Officer — ma maamulo bixinta lacagaha maalinlaha ah, laakiin 
wuu arki karaa budgets-ka guud.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A.2 FACULTY COORDINATOR (Isku-duwaha Faculty-ga)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Magaca nidaamka: faculty_coordinator

Sharaxaad:
Isku-duwaha faculty-ga wuxuu matalaa qaybta / department-ka uu ka tirsan yahay. 
Wuxuu ka shaqeeyaa heer faculty, ma aha heer jaamacadeed oo dhan.

Mas’uuliyadaha ugu muhiimsan:
  • Dib-u-eegista hore ee proposal-ka (pre-review) ka dib marka researcher gudbiyo
  • Hubinta in proposal-ku u qalmo in uu u gudbo Director
  • Ansixinta / diidmada publications-ka (validation) ee faculty-giisa
  • Horumarinta publication workflow — hal marxalad hal mar (Submitted → In process → Pipeline → Published)
  • Eegista Research Workflow Status ee researchers-ka faculty-giisa
  • Faculty dashboard & reports

Modules-ka uu geli karo:
Dashboard, Research Workflow Status, Ethics, Proposals, Projects, Publications, 
Thesis, Grants, Repository, Collaboration, Groups, Messages, Notifications, Profile.

Xaddidaad:
Wuxuu arkaa kaliya publications iyo data-ka ku xiran department-kiisa (marka la dejiyo).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A.3 FINANCE OFFICER (Sarkaalka Maaliyadda)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Magaca nidaamka: finance_officer

Sharaxaad:
Sarkaalka maaliyaddu wuxuu maamulaa lacagta, miisaaniyadda, bixinta, iyo dib u eegista
Purchase Orders ka hor Director.

Mas’uuliyadaha ugu muhiimsan:
  • Grant funding approval → Authorize budget (qoondee, ha bixin weli)
  • Maamulka budgets-ka iyo payment requests
  • Dib u eegista Purchase Orders (ansixi/diid) ka hor Director
  • Bixinta lacagaha / PO ka dib Director ansixiyo
  • Finance reports iyo donor reports
  • Project closure (finance sign-off)

Modules-ka uu geli karo:
Dashboard, Funding Calls, Grants, Finance & Budgets, Finance Reports, Donor Reports,
Collaboration, Messages, Notifications, Profile.

Socodka PO:
  Researcher → Finance (review) → Director → Finance (pay)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A.4 RESEARCHER (Cilmi-baaraha)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Magaca nidaamka: researcher

Sharaxaad:
Cilmi-baaruhu waa isticmaale sameeya cilmi-baarista — arday ama macalin / 
faculty member. Waa qofka bilaaba socodka cilmi-baarista.

Mas’uuliyadaha ugu muhiimsan:
  • Sameynta proposal + buuxinta foomka Ethics (REC)
  • Gudbinta proposal + ethics Director-ka
  • Maamulka project-kiisa ka dib ansixinta
  • Codsiga grant / funding
  • Diiwaangelinta publications & outputs
  • Soo gelinta files repository-ga
  • Ka qaybgalka groups, thesis, collaboration, messages

Modules-ka uu geli karo:
Dashboard, Research Workflow Status, Ethics, Proposals, Projects, Publications, 
Thesis, Grants, Budgets (budget-kiisa), Repository, Collaboration, Groups, 
Messages, Notifications, Profile.

Xaddidaad:
Wuxuu arkaa kaliya xogtiisa (My proposals, My projects, My outputs). Ma beddeli 
karo publication workflow stage — taasi waa Coordinator/Director.

QAYBTA B: RESEARCH WORKFLOW STATUS — PIPELINE-KA GUUD (11 TALLAABO)
================================================================================
Bogga: /research-workflow → Qaybta "Research pipeline"

Tallaabo kasta waxay leedahay midab / xaalad:

  ✓ Done (Cagaar)        — Waa la dhammeeyay
  → Current step (Buluug) — Hadda waa kan aad ku jirto
  ○ Pending (Cawl)       — Wali ma imaan
  ✕ Blocked (Guduud)     — Waa la diiday / xannibay
  — Skipped              — Looma baahna (tusaale ethics aan loo baahnayn)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.1 Create research proposal
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Researcher wuxuu abuuraa proposal cusub (title, abstract, department, 
research area, document). Haddii ethics loo baahan yahay, foomka REC waa la buuxiyaa 
isla bogga.

Yaa sameeyaa: Researcher
Xaalad marka la bilaabo: Done (marka proposal la abuuro)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.2 Ethics (REC) approval
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Research Ethics (JUREC/REC) waa in Director ansixiyo cilmi-baarista ka 
hor inta aan la gudbin si rasmi ah. Foomka REC wuxuu ka kooban yahay PI name, 
project level, aims, design, signature, iwm.

Yaa ansixiyaa: Research Director (ethics_committee role waa laga saaray)
Xaaladaha ethicsStatus: pending → approved / rejected
Haddii requiresEthics = false: Skipped

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.3 Submit proposal for review
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Researcher wuxuu guud ahaan u gudbiyaa proposal + ethics Director-ka 
(hal badhan). Proposal status wuxuu noqdaa "submitted".

Yaa sameeyaa: Researcher
Shuruud: Ethics form waa in uu dhammaystiran yahay (haddii loo baahan yahay)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.4 Faculty coordinator pre-review
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Isku-duwaha faculty-ga wuxuu dib u eegaa proposal-ka, wuxuu ku darayaa 
faallo, wuxuuna u diyaariyaa Director.

Yaa sameeyaa: Faculty Coordinator
Proposal status: submitted → under_review

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.5 Director decision
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Director wuxuu qaataa go’aanka ugu dambeeya:
  • Approved — proposal waa la ansixiyay, project waa la abuuri karaa
  • Rejected — waa la diiday (Blocked)
  • Revision requested — researcher waa in uu wax ka beddelaa

Yaa sameeyaa: Research Director
Shuruud: Ethics waa in ay horay u ansixisay (REC approved)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.6 Active research project
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Marka proposal la ansixiyo, mashruuc cilmi-baariseed (project) ayaa 
la abuuraa. Researcher wuxuu geli karaa milestones, team, progress updates.

Xaaladaha project: active | completed | on_hold
Yaa maamulaa: Researcher (progress), Director (oversee)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.7 Grant / funding request
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Researcher wuxuu codsan karaa deeq lacageed mashruuciisa.
Grant status marka hore: draft → submitted

Yaa sameeyaa: Researcher (codsiga)
Yaa ansixiyaa: Research Director

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.8 Grant awarded
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Director wuxuu ansixiyaa ama diidaa deeqda. Marka la ansixiyo, 
amountAwarded waa la dejiyaa.

Grant status: approved / active (deeq la siiyay)
Marka grant la ansixiyo: Budget waa la abuuri karaa si toos ah

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.9 Budget allocated
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Miisaaniyadda mashruuca waa la dejiyaa ka dib marka grant la siiyo.
Finance Officer wuxuu maamulaa bixinta lacagaha.

Yaa arkaa: Researcher (budget-kiisa), Finance Officer (maamulka)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.10 Research publication
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Researcher wuxuu diiwaangeliyaa natiijada cilmi-baarista (paper, 
journal, conference, thesis, iwm.) module-ka Publications.

Publication status: draft → submitted → validated / rejected
Wuxuu ku xiran yahay publication workflow (Qaybta C hoose)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B.11 Archive in repository
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Diiwaanka ugu dambeeya — files, reports, ama outputs waa la kaydiyaa 
Repository-ga jaamacadda si loo ilaaliyo loona heli karo mustaqbalka.

Yaa sameeyaa: Researcher (upload), Director/Coordinator (eegista)

================================================================================
QAYBTA C: PUBLICATION WORKFLOW (4 MARXALADOOD)
================================================================================
Bogga: /research-workflow → Qaybta "Publication workflow"

Kani waa socodka gaarka ah ee outputs-ka (publications) ka dib marka la gudbiyo.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
C.1 Submitted (📤 La gudbiyay)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Researcher wuxuu gudbiyay publication validation. Waxaa sugaya 
Faculty Coordinator ama Director.

Yaa sameeyaa: Researcher (gudbinta)
Xaalad: First stage after submit

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
C.2 In process (⚙️ Socda)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Faculty wuxuu dib u eegayaa output-ka, wuxuu hubinayaa in uu u qalmo 
in uu galo pipeline-ka daabacaadda.

Yaa horumariyaa: Faculty Coordinator (hal marxalad) ama Director

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
C.3 Pipeline (📋 Jidka daabacaadda)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Output-ku wuxuu ku jiraa habka daabacaadda — journal submission, 
conference review, ama hab kale oo daabacaadeed.

Yaa horumariyaa: Faculty Coordinator / Director

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
C.4 Published (✅ La daabacay)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sharaxaad: Output-ku waa la daabacay ama si rasmi ah loo ansixiyay. System-ku 
wuxuu si toos ah u beddelaa publication status → validated.

Yaa horumariyaa: Faculty Coordinator / Director
Natiijo: Researcher wuxuu helaa notification

Xeerka horumarinta:
  • Coordinator: hal marxalad hal mar (Submitted → In process → Pipeline → Published)
  • Director: wuu horumarin karaa marxaladaha si toos ah

QAYBTA D: XAALADAHA PROPOSAL (PROPOSAL STATUSES)
================================================================================

  draft              — Qabyo; researcher wuu wax ka beddeli karaa
  submitted          — La gudbiyay; sugaya coordinator review
  under_review       — Coordinator / Director way dib u eegayaan
  approved           — La ansixiyay; project waa la abuuri karaa
  rejected           — La diiday
  revision_requested — Wax ka beddel ayaa la codsaday

================================================================================
QAYBTA E: XAALADAHA ETHICS / REC (ETHICS STATUSES)
================================================================================

Proposal ethicsStatus:
  not_required       — Anshax looma baahna proposal-kan
  pending            — Sugaya ansixin
  approved           — REC waa ansixisay
  rejected           — REC waa diiday
  revision_requested — Wax ka beddel ayaa la codsaday

Ethics Application (standalone) status:
  draft     — Qabyo
  submitted — La gudbiyay
  approved  — La ansixiyay (certificate issued)
  rejected  — La diiday

================================================================================
QAYBTA F: XAALADAHA GRANT (GRANT STATUSES)
================================================================================

  draft     — Qabyo; researcher wuu qorayaa codsiga
  submitted — La gudbiyay Director-ka
  approved  — La ansixiyay
  rejected  — La diiday
  active    — Deeq firfircoon / la siiyay
  closed    — La xiray / dhammaatay

================================================================================
QAYBTA G: XAALADAHA PROJECT (PROJECT STATUSES)
================================================================================

  active    — Mashruuc socda
  completed — La dhammeeyay
  on_hold   — La hakiyay

================================================================================
QAYBTA H: XAALADAHA PUBLICATION VALIDATION
================================================================================

  draft     — Qabyo; kaliya researcher buu arkaa
  submitted — La gudbiyay validation
  validated — La ansixiyay (Faculty / system)
  rejected  — La diiday

Noocyada publication-ka:
  paper, journal_article, conference, book, book_chapter, patent, thesis, 
  review, case_study, letter_to_editor, community_research_impact

QAYBTA I: SOCDKA GUUD — TUSAALE RESEARCHER
================================================================================

1. Researcher wuxuu galaa RMS → dooranayaa UG ama PG portal
2. Wuxuu abuuraa Proposal + buuxiyaa Ethics form (REC)
3. Wuxuu gudbiyaa Director-ka
4. Faculty Coordinator wuu dib u eegaa
5. Director wuu ansixiyaa ethics, kadib wuu ansixiyaa proposal
6. Project waa la abuuraa — researcher wuu socodsiiyaa cilmi-baarista
7. Wuxuu codsanayaa Grant → Director ansixiyaa → Budget la dejiyaa
8. Wuxuu diiwaangeliyaa Publication → Coordinator horumariyaa workflow
9. Wuxuu kaydiyaa Repository-ga
10. Research Workflow Status wuxuu muujinayaa halka uu joogo tallaabo kasta

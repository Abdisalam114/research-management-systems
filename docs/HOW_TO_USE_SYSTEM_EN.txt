1. OVERVIEW

JUST RMS manages university research from funding calls through proposals, multi-stage review, project execution, finance, publications, and formal closure.

Two portals exist: Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG). Only the Research Director can switch between them. Data never mixes.

Open the app at http://localhost:5173 after starting backend (:5000) and frontend (:5173).


2. USER JOBS — WHAT EACH ROLE DOES (8)

There is no separate Ethics Committee or Peer Reviewer login. Ethics = Research Director. Peer review = University Leadership.

Research Director (director@rms.edu)
  • Switch UG/PG portals; create/publish funding calls (no Leadership step)
  • Final Approve/Reject proposals; decide ethics (JUREC)
  • Approve POs after Procurement; manage Users & Departments
  • Analytics, KPI, audit trail

University Leadership (leadership@ / leadership.pg@)
  • Peer review: score 1–5 + comment on assigned proposals
  • Peer Reviews + Proposals menus; KPI
  • Does NOT publish funding calls

Faculty Coordinator (coordinator@ / coordinator.pg@)
  • Faculty proposal queue; thesis groups (min 4 students), titles, supervisors
  • Publication workflow; Validate → Published
  • Faculty monitoring / reports

Finance Officer (finance@ / finance.pg@) — MONEY (not Procurement)
  • Grant funding approval → Authorize budget (allocate only, not pay)
  • Proposal finance review; budgets; payment requests; pay approved items
  • Pay POs after Director approval; finance reports; finance closure
  • Does NOT decide what to buy (that is Procurement)

Procurement Officer (procurement@ / procurement.pg@) — BUYING / PO (not Finance)
  • Review Purchase Orders from researchers
  • Approve/reject PO before Director
  • Does NOT authorize grants; does NOT pay money
  • Flow: Researcher PO → Procurement → Director → Finance pays

HR Officer (hr@ / hr.pg@)
  • View projects, thesis, groups for HR context
  • No grant/ethics/payment approvals

Donor Agency (donor@ / donor.pg@)
  • Draft external funding calls; wait for Leadership/Director publish
  • Donor reports; monitor related grants

Researcher (asha@ UG / mahad@ PG)
  • Apply to calls; write proposals (budget + ethics + docs)
  • Run projects; budgets; payment requests; create POs
  • Publications Create & submit; repository; thesis supervision; closure checklist

Director chooses UG or PG after login. All others use one portal automatically.

Do not use ethics@ or reviewer@ — those roles were removed.


4. FUNDING CALLS

Director creates Internal or External calls. Required documents auto-fill. Choose eligibility (UG / PG / all / PGD).

Director publishes the call themselves — Leadership is not required. Eligible researchers get notifications with a direct link.

Researcher: Funding Calls → Apply via this call.


5. REVIEW WORKFLOW

Admin screening → Leadership peer review (score 1–5 + comment) → Committee → Ethics (Research Director) → Finance review → Director Approve.

Approved funding-call proposals create a Project and queue a Grant for finance (status: pending_finance).


6. AUTHORIZE BUDGET (FINANCE)

Path: Sidebar → Grant funding approval (/finance/grant-approvals)

Login as finance@rms.edu (UG) or finance.pg@rms.edu (PG).

Click Authorize budget. This ALLOCATES the budget only — it does not pay money. Disburse later under Finance & Budgets.


7. PUBLICATIONS

One output per project (1:1). Researcher Create & submit → status submitted.

Coordinator/Director Validate → status validated and workflow stage Published.

You can also advance Submitted → In process → Pipeline → Published on Research Workflow Status.

Use the Published filter to find published outputs (do not leave Submitted filter selected).


8. FULL JOURNEY

Funding call → Grant → Proposal (budget + ethics + docs) → Review → Project → Finance authorize → Work plan + budgets → Payments + PO → Publications (Validate = Published) → Closure checklist → Archived.


9. KEY MENUS

Funding Calls, Grants, Proposals, Peer Reviews, Projects, Publications, Research Workflow, Finance & Budgets, Grant funding approval (Finance only), Policies, Search, Notifications, Audit Trail (Director/Coordinator).

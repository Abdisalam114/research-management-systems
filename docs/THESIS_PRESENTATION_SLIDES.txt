APPENDIX A — ABSTRACT (English, for thesis document)
================================================================================

Title: Design and Implementation of a Web-Based Research Management System
       for Jamhuriya University

Abstract:

This thesis presents the design, development, and evaluation of the Jamhuriya
Research Management System (RMS), a web-based platform built using the MERN
stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js) to digitize the full research
lifecycle at Jamhuriya University. The system addresses the limitations of
manual, paper-based research administration by providing an integrated
platform that connects four user roles — Research Director, Faculty Research
Coordinator, Finance Officer, and Researcher — across separate Undergraduate
and Postgraduate portals.

The RMS implements eight functional modules: research proposal management
(including ethics approval and document version control), project management,
grant and funding management, publication tracking, budget and financial
management, institutional repository (with OAI-PMH export), research
collaboration, and institutional analytics with PDF report generation.

Beyond the eight core modules, the implementation adds dedicated modules for
ethics review (REC) and thesis supervision (ThesisGroup: supervisors, students,
meetings, and status tracking). Final thesis documents are also recorded as
publication outputs and repository items.

Key automated workflows include automatic project creation upon proposal
approval and automatic budget allocation upon grant award. Role-based access
control and JWT authentication ensure data security. The system was tested
using realistic seed data (200+ documents), manual role-based verification,
and automated smoke test scripts. All core workflows operate correctly
end-to-end. The system is deployable on free-tier cloud infrastructure.

Keywords: Research Management System, MERN stack, MongoDB, RBAC, University
Research Workflow, Institutional Repository, Jamhuriya University
